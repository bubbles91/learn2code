I definitely won't be passing any cars.

If you don't know what a Berma Shave style sign is, here is a little info to help.

Berma Shave Signs
Road signs common in the U.S. in the 1920's and 1930's advertising shaving products. Signs were grouped in bunches of four, five or six, each with one line from the slogan.
